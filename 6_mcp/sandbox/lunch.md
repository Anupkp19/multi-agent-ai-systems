# Lunch Recipe: Egg Fried Rice

**Approximate Cost:** under ₹200  
**Serves:** 2-3 people  
**Total Time:** 30 minutes  

## Ingredients:

- 1 cup rice (uncooked)
- 2 large eggs
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 1 carrot, diced (optional)
- ¼ cup green peas (optional)
- 2-3 tablespoons oil
- 2-3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 1-2 green chilies, chopped (optional)
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- Salt to taste
- Pepper to taste
- Spring onions for garnish (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Cook Rice:**  
   Cook rice as per packet instructions. Spread it on a plate to cool (best if leftover or at least cooled).

2. **Scramble Eggs:**  
   Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan. Beat eggs with a pinch of salt, pour into the pan, and scramble. Remove and set aside.

3. **Sauté Veggies:**  
   Add the rest of the oil to the same pan. Add garlic and onion, sauté until translucent. Add carrot and peas (if using), cook until they soften.

4. **Combine:**  
   Add cooled rice to the pan. Mix well and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.

5. **Add Seasoning:**  
   Pour in soy sauce, add salt and pepper. Stir well.

6. **Finish Up:**  
   Add scrambled eggs back to the pan, mix everything thoroughly. Garnish with chopped spring onion if desired.

7. **Serve Hot!**

---

**Tips:**  
- Leftover rice works best for fried rice.
- You can add any veggies you have on hand within budget.
- Adjust spices as per your taste.

---

This meal is filling, tasty, and keeps well for a lunchbox!
